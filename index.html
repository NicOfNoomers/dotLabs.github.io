Budget Creation

Users can easily create personalized budgets based on their income, expenses, and financial goals. They can set specific categories and allocate funds accordingly.

Expense Tracking

The web app allows users to track their expenses in real-time. They can input transactions manually or sync their accounts to automatically import and categorize transactions.

Users can set short-term and long-term financial goals, such as saving for a vacation or paying off debt. The app provides progress tracking and sends alerts when goals are achieved.

Dot Labs' web apps utilize interactive charts and graphs to visually represent financial data. Users can gain a clear understanding of their spending patterns and identify areas for improvement.

Customizable Categories

Users have the flexibility to create custom categories to match their unique spending habits and preferences. This ensures accurate tracking and analysis of expenses.

Expense Analysis

The app provides detailed expense breakdowns, allowing users to identify trends, highlight areas of overspending, and make informed decisions to optimize their budgets.
